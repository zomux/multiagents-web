WMAC 2025 Panel Discussion Topics

Exploring the Future of LLM-Based Multi-Agent Collaboration

  1. Communication Protocols and Knowledge Sharing: How do we design effective protocols for inter-agent communication and knowledge sharing? What mechanisms enable agents to share and update their knowledge bases while balancing expressiveness, efficiency, and specialization?
  2. Trust and Verification: How can we build trust in multi-agent systems? What mechanisms should be in place to verify the accuracy and reliability of inter-agent communications?
  3. Responsible AI in Multi-Agent Systems: How do we ensure responsible development and deployment of multi-agent systems? What governance frameworks and safeguards should be in place to ensure alignment with human values and societal benefits?
  4. Learning in Multi-Agent Environments: What are the unique challenges and opportunities in multi-agent learning compared to single-agent learning? How can we effectively train agents to collaborate?
  5. Human-AI Collaboration: How do multi-agent systems change the dynamics of human-AI collaboration? What new interfaces and interaction patterns are needed?
  6. Coordinating Agents with Different/Competing Goals: How do we effectively manage and orchestrate agents with diverse or conflicting objectives? What mechanisms can help balance individual agent goals with system-level objectives?
  7. Modeling Uncertainty in Large Scale Multi-agent Environments: How do we effectively model and handle uncertainties in complex multi-agent systems? What approaches can help maintain system stability and reliability at scale?
  8. Emergent Behaviors in Multi-Agent Systems: How do we understand and control emergent behaviors when multiple LLM-based agents interact? What are the implications for safety and reliability?
  9. Applications of LLM-based Multi-Agent Collaboration: What are the most promising real-world applications and use cases for LLM-based multi-agent systems? How do we identify domains where multi-agent approaches provide significant advantages over single-agent solutions?
  10. Future Directions: What are the most promising research directions for advancing multi-agent collaboration? What are the key technical and societal challenges we need to address?
