WMAC 2025 - Session Chair Handbook

Internal guidance document for session chairs

Prepare

Before the Workshop

  • Arrive 30 minutes before the first session
  • Check A/V equipment functionality
  • Ensure timer display is working
  • Double check that you have backup copies of all presentations
  • Check remote participation / virtual workshop setup; figure out how virtual attendees are going to participate in the workshop
  • Locate the A/V support team from AAAI
  • Figure out how the slides will be displayed on the screen, do they allow presenters to plugin their laptop or we have to display the slides from our side?
  • Figure out how the posters will be displayed, and see wether we need to pre-assign the poster submission number to each easel to avoid confusion
  • Two organizers to sign on the best paper award, you can do it using the lunch time
