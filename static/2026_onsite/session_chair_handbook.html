WMAC 2026 - Session Chair Handbook

Internal guidance document for session chairs

Room: Topaz 220 – 225

Prepare

Before the Workshop

  • Arrive 30 minutes before the first session
  • Check A/V equipment functionality
  • Ensure timer display is working
  • Double check that you have backup copies of all presentations
  • Check remote participation / virtual workshop setup; figure out how virtual attendees are going to participate in the workshop
  • Locate the A/V support team from AAAI
  • Figure out how the slides will be displayed on the screen, do they allow presenters to plugin their laptop or we have to display the slides from our side?
  • Figure out how the posters will be displayed, and see whether we need to pre-assign the poster submission number to each easel to avoid confusion
  • Prepare the best paper award certificate during lunch time
  • Catered lunch boxes need to arrive by 11am
  • Prepare goodies for attendees: T-shirts and tote bags

Volunteer Assignments (5 volunteers)

Role Assignments

  • Volunteer 1 - Room Runner: Help with microphone for Q&A, assist speakers with laptop/slides, coordinate with A/V team
  • Volunteer 2 - Time Keeper: Show time cards to speakers (5 min, 2 min, STOP), keep sessions on schedule
  • Volunteer 3 - Photographer: Take photos of speakers, sessions, poster session, and group photo
  • Volunteer 4 - Usher: Direct attendees to the room, guide latecomers, help with seating, assist during lunch distribution
  • Volunteer 5 - Check-in Table: Check in attendees, distribute goodies (T-shirts, tote bags)

Before the Workshop (All volunteers)

  • Arrive 30 minutes before the workshop starts
  • Help set up check-in table at the entrance
  • Arrange goodies for distribution
  • Help set up poster easels

After the Workshop (All volunteers)

  • Help presenters remove posters
  • Collect leftover materials and goodies
  • General cleanup of the room
