Key Insights from 2025

  • LLMs facilitate collaboration comparable to MARL methods
  • Sparse communication reduces costs by ~40%
  • Collaborative frameworks achieve ~47% better task completion
  • Challenges remain: long-context handling, hallucinations
  • Theory of Mind capabilities are limited but present
